MindBreaker is a 2D action-adventure game set in a dystopian future where an advanced AI named Cerebrus has taken control of the world. Your mission is to rescue your brother, the AI's original creator, who has been captured. Only he holds the key to freeing humanity from Cerebrus’ grip.

Game Overview

Story: Fight through Cerebrus’ robot minions to save your brother and ultimately defeat the AI overlord.

Mechanics: Destroy hostile robots while managing your sanity—robots drain your sanity when near, affecting your perception and abilities.

Setting: Futuristic, cyberpunk-style 2D environments with intense combat and psychological elements.

Technical Details

Engine: Unity (2D)

Language: C#

Core Systems:

Player movement and combat

Enemy AI with proximity-based sanity drain mechanic

Sanity system affecting gameplay visuals and player controls

Level progression with story-driven missions

UI with health, sanity meter, and mission objectives

Physics: 2D Rigidbody and collision detection for smooth movement and interactions.

Animation: Spritesheet animations using Unity Animator.

Audio: Dynamic sound effects that respond to sanity state changes and combat.

Input: Supports keyboard and gamepad controls.
